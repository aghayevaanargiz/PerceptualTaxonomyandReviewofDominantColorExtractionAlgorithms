## Overview

This paper presents a comprehensive survey of perceptually-aware dominant color extraction (DCE) methods. We propose a novel taxonomy that categorizes DCE approaches into three paradigms:

| Paradigm | Core Principle |
|----------|----------------|
| **Perception-Aware Clustering** | Extends statistical clustering with perceptual color spaces (CIELAB, LCH) and saliency weighting |
| **Fuzzy Approaches** | Models perceptual uncertainty through fuzzy membership and categorical reasoning |
| **Deep Learning Techniques** | Learns semantic context and human perceptual patterns from data |

---

## Key Contributions

- **Novel dual-axis taxonomy** structured by algorithmic paradigm and level of perceptual integration
- **Comparative analysis** of principles, strengths, and limitations across three methodological paradigms
- **Evaluation framework** reviewing ground truth generation, metrics, and benchmarking protocols
- **Guidance framework** for selecting DCE methods based on application constraints (efficiency, interpretability, perceptual fidelity)
